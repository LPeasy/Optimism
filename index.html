What the checklist tests

The goal is not more AI paperwork. The goal is a workflow a manager can explain, approve, stop, and improve.

Scope

Can the team name one workflow, output, owner, and review point?

Data

Are approved inputs, prohibited data, and retention rules defined?

Tools

Are allowed tools, blocked actions, and access limits clear?

Evidence

Can material claims and exceptions be traced to source records?

Approval

Does a named human approve output before business action?

Recovery

Are stop conditions, incidents, and handoff rules documented?
